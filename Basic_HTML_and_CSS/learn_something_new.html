Vaccine Information for Puppies

Puppies get 3 rounds of vaccines. First round is usually the DHPPV and a dewormer, second round is DHPPV with Lepto, Bordetella and again dewormer, final round DHPPV with Lepto, Rabies, and dewormer. The schedule is 8weeks, 12 weeks, 16 weeks.
